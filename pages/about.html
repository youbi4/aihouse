About Us
Crafted with passion and dedication to excellence, our team brings unique perspectives and specialized skills.
We are committed to creating lasting value and positive impact in all our endeavors.

Who We Are

The AI House is a scientific and technological space that offers a lively, inspiring co-working area for students, professors, and researchers in the field of artificial intelligence, as well as for innovative start-up founders, micro-project owners, entrepreneurs, and anyone interested in this vital domain.

We enable everyone to exchange ideas, experiences, and expertise, and we nurture young talents to take their first steps in a technology that captures the world's attention. We also organise meetings with specialists, experts, and economic stakeholders to strengthen the ecosystem.

Our History

The AI House of Hassiba Benbouali University of Chlef (MIA) was founded in 2024 as the first open academic space bringing together students, professors, researchers, and entrepreneurs. It was born from the university's ambition to create a hub of excellence in artificial intelligence, becoming a vibrant co-working and inspirational platform.

The House was inspired by the need for a technical meeting point connecting developers, experts, and start-ups, providing a dynamic environment to take the first steps into the world of AI, while preserving the Algerian identity and maintaining international openness.

Our Mission

  • Introduce AI to professors, students, and economic enterprises.
  • Raise awareness about ethical AI use and its contribution to the national economy.
  • Strengthen the university's scientific standing through the latest AI technologies.
  • Drive the university's digital transformation (administrative, pedagogical, research).
  • Collaborate closely with the incubator to support graduate students and project leaders.

Our Vision

We aspire for the AI House to be a bridge between researchers, developers, and economic enterprises, turning scientific research into innovative products and services. We work to introduce AI technologies into vital sectors: renewable energies, environment, agriculture, small and medium enterprises, health, and education.

We aim to contribute to the development of international standards and government policies in the field of AI, and to anchor a culture of open innovation.

Our Objectives

  • Incubate smart software developers (especially students) and support them to realise their projects.
  • Support and encourage research in AI and its applications across various fields.
  • Train trainers in the different branches of artificial intelligence.
  • Provide consulting and expertise to students, researchers, and economic operators.
  • Establish partnerships with economic enterprises to develop smart applications and turn research into products.
  • Contribute to government policies and international AI standards.
